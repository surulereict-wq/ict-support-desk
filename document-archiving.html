SmartAce FAQ

I'm not yet registered — can I still log a complaint?

No. Select "New registration" first; complaints are tied to an existing SmartAce profile and Staff ID.

How long until a payslip correction reflects?

Corrections are typically reviewed within the standard turnaround, but may reflect in the following pay cycle depending on when payroll is processed.
