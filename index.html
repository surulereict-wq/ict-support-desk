ICT Unit · Internal Support

Raise a support request with the ICT Unit

Select the service that matches your issue below. Each request is logged with a reference number so you can follow up with the desk directly.

Support categories

CU · 01

Computer Upgrade Session

Book a session for RAM/storage upgrades, hardware diagnostics, or full workstation replacement.

Typical turnaround: 2–3 working days

 PR · 02

Printer Ink Outage & Repairs

Report an out-of-ink printer, paper jams, or mechanical faults on office printers.

Typical turnaround: Same day – 24 hrs

 SW · 03

Software Upgrade

Request an OS update, application install, license renewal, or security patch.

Typical turnaround: 1–2 working days

 SA · 04

SmartAce Registration & Complaint

Register on SmartAce or report payslip and official documentation issues.

Typical turnaround: 3–5 working days

 DA · 05

Document Archiving & Retrieval

Submit files for digital archiving, or request retrieval of an already-archived record.

Typical turnaround: 2–4 working days

Before you submit

What happens after I submit a request?

You'll receive a reference number on screen immediately. Keep it — it's how the desk tracks and prioritises your request internally.

Can I submit on behalf of my department?

Yes. Use your department's official name and a phone extension or email that the ICT Unit can reach during working hours.

What if my issue doesn't fit any category?

Choose the closest match and describe the issue clearly in the details field — the desk will redirect it internally if needed.
